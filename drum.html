Drum

The drum is a member of the percussion group of musical instruments. In the simplest terms, it is a hollow cylinder that produces sound when struck with a hand, stick, or mallet. Drums are an essential part of various musical styles such as rock, jazz, and pop music.

Cool Facts about Drum:

  1. The world's oldest known drum dates back to 5500 BC and was discovered in China.
  2. The largest drum set in the world consists of 340 pieces.
  3. The drummer with the fastest hands is Mike Mangini, who set a world record of 1,203 single-stroke hits in 60 seconds.
