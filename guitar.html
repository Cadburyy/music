Guitar

The guitar is a fretted musical instrument that typically has six strings. It is played by strumming or plucking the strings with the fingers, thumb, or a pick. It is widely used in various musical genres such as blues, country, rock, and many others.

Cool Facts about Guitar:

  1. The modern guitar as we know it has been around since the 15th century.
  2. There are many different types of guitars, including acoustic, electric, and bass guitars.
  3. The world's largest playable guitar stands at 13.23 meters (43 feet 5.25 inches) tall.
