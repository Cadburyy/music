Piano

The piano is a musical instrument that is played by pressing keys on a keyboard. It is one of the most popular and versatile musical instruments in the world. The piano has a wide range and is used in various musical genres such as classical, jazz, and contemporary music.

Cool Facts about Piano:

  1. The piano was invented around the year 1700 by an Italian, Bartolomeo Cristofori.
  2. The word "piano" is a shortened form of the word "pianoforte," which means "soft-loud" in Italian.
  3. The world's largest piano, known as the Klavins 370, stands at a towering 12 feet 1 inch (3.7 meters) tall.
